Rocket Shot

Welcome

The goal of the game is to defeat Mr.Kartoshka and free yourself from his insidious influence. The player must go through various levels and challenges to solve riddles and collect the keys needed to unlock the final battle. During the course of the game, the player will fight against enemies such as the Rare Tiran and the Uncommon Schalun. He will also face various obstacles that Mr.Kartoshka and his minions will create. The player will also be able to collect various items and upgrades that will help him in the battle with Mr.Kartoshka. Ultimately, the player must fight to defeat Mr.Kartoshka and restore peace and freedom in this exciting adventure.

Mr.Kartoshka

Story

The legendary Mister Kartoshka is a great genius with an unbridled mind, capable of subjugating anyone. His genius and cunning allow him to manipulate everyone around him.

Dimastus

Story

Epic Dimastus - An ordinary guy striving for wealth. In search of easy money, he met the Mysterious Kartoshka, who turned his life upside down. Now he has become a pawn in the hands of this insidious villain.

Tiran

Story

Rare Tyrant (Dog) - Once a friendly and affectionate dog who loved the simple pleasures of life. But the appearance of Kartoshka and Dimastus changed him forever. Now he is Kartoshka`s servant, carrying out his evil orders.

Shalun

Story

Unusual Shalun - A mysterious figure who has been watching what is happening for a long time. Inspired by the game and jokes, he decided to join Kartoshka team and become another subordinate.

Karasik

Story

Common Karasik - An innocent fish that found itself in a trap set by Kartoshka, Dimastus and the Dog-Tiran. She became an unwitting witness to their atrocities, becoming a tiny link in their sinister plan.
